Why we're here

We believe caring for the earth starts close to home.

Earth Sweet Earth began with a simple idea: young people don't have to wait to make a difference. Small actions — a cleaner park, a planted tree, a friend who learns something new — add up to real change.

We're building a community of people who want to help our planet feel a little more like home for everyone who shares it.

"Nobody's too small, and no place is too ordinary, to be worth taking care of."

What we're growing

A few of the things we care about.

01

Neighborhood clean-ups

We pick a park, a beach, or a stretch of sidewalk and clean it up together — then step back and enjoy how much better it looks.

02

Learning by sharing

We turn what we learn about the environment into simple guides and honest conversations, so helping the planet feels doable for anyone.

03

Planting & greener spaces

We plant trees and tend small gardens that give people shade, wildlife a home, and the neighborhood a little more life.
